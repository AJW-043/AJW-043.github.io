About Me

From physics to basket weaving and back

Who am I?

I'm currently a bachelors student at the University College Utrecht majoring in physics, math and chemistry, with a minor in economics. In the future I hope to apply my knowledge in physics to medical physics or climate physics, two topics I feel very drawn to.

Outside of academics I've been involved in sustainable start-ups and growing the alumni internship network at UCU. Through alumni connections I hope to help fellow students develop professional and hands-on experience. In my free time you'll find me hiking, camping, playing volleyball or guitar, solving puzzles, or, yes, sometimes even weaving a basket.

Brief Background

Growing up, I lived in Austria, Sri Lanka and the Czech Republic. Being exposed to different cultures at a young age helped me develop a more balanced world view, and understand environmental and health related problems from different perspectives.
